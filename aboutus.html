ABOUT US

Want to know more about us and what we do. We will satisfy your curiosity.

BET ON BETTER works to better the state of living of orphan children, save lives, preserve nature, wildlife and trees. We are there to provide life-saving assistance when pandemic strikes- like COVID-19 and to help people rebuild their lives afterwards.And we are there to work alongside other people and communities on long-term programmes to deliver lasting change.

Our programmes and our policy work tackle the underlying causes of decreasing forest cover so that we can make more efficient plans. We place special focus on promoting blood donation as many people with ailments require it.

Our mission

Save lives, preserve wildlife and trees.

Our vision

We seek a world of hope, where trees and wildlife is preserved and nurtured.

Our focus

We promote blood donation and seek the causes for decreasing forest cover so that we can efficiently plan forward for our future generations.

Our values

Respect - We affirm the dignity, potential and contribution of participants, donors, partners and staff.

Integrity - Our actions are consistent with our mission. We are honest and transparent in what we do and say, and accept responsibility for our collective and individual actions.

Commitment - We work together effectively to serve the larger community.

Excellence - We constantly challenge ourselves to the highest levels of learning and performance to achieve greater impact.

Our principles

1. Demonstrate personal and professional integrity.

2. Deliver high quality development programmes that make a tangible difference.

3. Work with others to multiply our impact.

4. Be accountable and transparent.

5. Be responsible stewards of all of our resources.

TO PROVIDE A BETTER FUTURE WHERE EVERYONE IS THRIVING
