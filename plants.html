BILLION TREES

We aim to ensure one billion trees have been re-grown, saved from loss, and better protected
around the world by 2050.

Forests cover 31 percent of the global land area. The total forest area is 4.06 billion hectares, or approximately (50 x 100m) per person. Over the areas these forest areas have been rapidly decreasing to the extent that some plant species have gone extinct or are on the brink of extinction. Our plan is to plant and grow 1 billion trees till 2050. Lend us a hand by donating to us.
